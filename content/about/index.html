Welcome to my personal website ! I use it as a platform to keep track of my projects, my programming notes, my research publications as well as my readings.

My name is Hugo Soubrier and I am from the South-East of France.

To make it short I love everything that is related to Nature and Biology, but especially Infectious diseases and Epidemiology to which I devoted my studies.

I have studied Biology & Microbiology at Imperial College London, before an MSc in Control of Infectious Diseases at the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine (LSHTM). Since my MSc, I have been working at Bernhard Nocht Institute of Tropical Medicine (BNITM) in the Africa team of the Virology department.

I am driven by the desire to do Field Epidemiology of Infectious diseases as a response to Outbreaks and Emergencies. I am passionate about the field work, but also data science and Epidemiology tools. As a result, I am working toward bridging both fields, and use the data science tools such as R, Rmarkdown, Shiny, SQL and others to the response of Outbreaks and the studies of Emerging infectious diseases on the field.

Being curious and interested in many aspects of Infectious diseases, I have been trying to accumulate knowledge and competence in various domains, with the perspective of becoming a good and complete Epidemiologist.
